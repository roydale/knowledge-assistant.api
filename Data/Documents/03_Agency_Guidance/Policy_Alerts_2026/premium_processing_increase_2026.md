# 2026 Inflation Adjustment: Premium Processing Fees

## 1. Summary of Changes
DHS is increasing premium processing fees to reflect inflation from June 2023 through June 2025 based on the Consumer Price Index (CPI-U).

## 2. New Fee Schedule (Effective March 1, 2026)
| Form Type | Current Fee (2025) | New Fee (March 2026) |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **I-129 (H-1B, L-1, etc.)** | $2,805 | $3,145 |
| **I-140 (EB-1, EB-2, EB-3)** | $2,805 | $3,145 |
| **I-539 (Change/Extend Status)**| $1,965 | $2,205 |
| **I-765 (EAD - OPT/STEM)** | $1,685 | $1,890 |

## 3. Compliance Instructions
- **Postmark Rule:** Any Form I-907 postmarked on or after March 1, 2026, **must** include the new fee amount or the request will be rejected.
- **Form Edition:** Ensure use of the most recent I-907 edition to avoid rejection.
