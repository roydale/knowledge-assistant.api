# EB-2 National Interest Waiver (NIW)

## 1. Eligibility Overview
Allows foreign nationals with "Exceptional Ability" or "Advanced Degrees" to skip the PERM labor certification process if their work is in the **U.S. National Interest**.

## 2. The Three-Prong Test (Matter of Dhanasar)
1. **Substantial Merit & National Importance:** The proposed endeavor must have significant impact (e.g., STEM, critical tech, or economic revitalization).
2. **Well-Positioned:** The applicant must have the background, skills, and record of success to advance the endeavor.
3. **Waiver Benefit:** It must be beneficial to the U.S. to waive the requirement of a job offer and labor certification.

## 3. 2026 Policy Notes
- **STEM Priority:** USCIS continues to favor Ph.D. holders in "Critical and Emerging Technologies" (AI, Quantum, Clean Energy).
- **Processing Times:** Standard processing has stretched to 20+ months. Premium processing (45 business days) is highly recommended for those with current priority dates.
- **2026 Visa Bulletin:** For January 2026, the "Dates for Filing" for most countries is **October 15, 2024**.
