# Orange Ice (Gelato di aranci)

## Ingredients
 - Four big oranges.
 - One lemon.
 - One pint of water.
 - Sugar, 3/4 lb.

## Instructions
Squeeze the oranges and the lemon and strain the juice.

Boil the sugar in the water for ten minutes, put in the juice when cold,
strain again and put in the freezer.
